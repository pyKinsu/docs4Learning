## Full curriculum

The C Language tab covers everything in order:

1. **Fundamentals** — identifiers, keywords, data types, variables, operators, control structures
2. **Functions** — user-defined, predefined, recursive
3. **Array** — 1D, 2D, multi-dimensional, arrays in functions
4. **String** — declaration, input, manipulation functions
5. **Structure & Union** — structs, unions, enums, self-referential structures
6. **Pointer** — declaration, arithmetic, pointers with arrays and structs
7. **File Handling** — read, write, append, sequential vs random access
8. **Preprocessor** — `#define`, macros, `#include`, conditional compilation
